Who We Are

Insurance Advocate

"We negotiate the most efficient policy terms, every time."

Clarkson Edu Insurance Brokers is one of Nigeria's most established and respected insurance advisory firms. Founded in 1962 in Lagos, we have spent over sixty years building a reputation for professionalism, technical depth, and a genuine commitment to the people and businesses we serve.

We are not an insurer; we are your advocate. As independent brokers, our loyalty is entirely to you. We analyse your exposures, source the most competitive policies, and stay by your side through claims and renewals.

Our brand has remained a worthy ambassador of the insurance profession, trusted by individuals, SMEs, and large corporates alike across a wide range of sectors.

Our Story

Born in an Era of Growth.
Built for the Long Term.

Clarkson was born in an era when insurance in Nigeria was still finding its footing. Over the decades, we have grown alongside the country's economy, adapting to regulatory change, embracing new risk categories, and consistently raising the bar for what professional insurance brokerage looks like.

Today, we manage diverse and complex insurance programs for clients spanning manufacturing, healthcare, financial services, real estate, and more. What has never changed is our founding principle: that every client deserves access to the right cover, properly negotiated, clearly explained.

Governance & Compliance

Operating to the Highest Regulatory Standards

Clarkson operates in strict compliance with the regulatory framework governing insurance intermediaries in Nigeria. Our Board of Directors provides experienced governance oversight, maintaining the high standards our clients and regulators expect.

We operate a zero-tolerance policy for legal and regulatory breaches, and uphold full transparency in all disclosures. We adhere to codes and directives issued by:
